Recommended Path

9 stops

Follow this sequence if you want one clean arc from hearing pitch relationships to shaping patterns, harmony, timbre, and form before situating those sounds inside a wider map of styles.

  1. Teoria Note Ear Training to lock in single-note recognition.
  2. Teoria Interval Ear Training to hear distance and contour between notes.
  3. Ableton Play with beats to start building rhythmic motion one bar at a time.
  4. Ableton Play with chords to add harmony and stronger progression cues.
  5. Ableton Learning Music Playground to combine beats, bass, chords, and melody in motion.
  6. Ableton Learning Synths: Get started to connect pitch and pattern work to a playable synth surface.
  7. How synths make sound to hear oscillators and waveform changes as a concrete sound source.
  8. Filter resonance to shape brightness, focus, and motion with a filter instead of only changing notes.
  9. Recipes to hear full subtractive-synth patches as combinations of the earlier controls.
  10. Musicmap to connect those sounds to a broader genre history.
